The Silent Crisis

Food Wasted Could Feed the World

While millions go to sleep with hollow stomachs, tonnes of perfectly good food is thrown away every day — from restaurants, events, and households. The gap between surplus and starvation is just a lack of connection.

FoodShare bridges that gap. We connect those who have with those who need, so that every meal finds its purpose — and every person finds their dignity.

The Process

How You Can Help

Three simple steps stand between surplus food and a family in need. Your action today becomes someone's meal tomorrow.

01
List Your Donation

Have surplus food from an event, your kitchen, or your business? List it in seconds — food type, quantity, location, and expiry. No food is too little.

02
NGOs Connect

Trusted NGOs and volunteers browse available donations and claim what they need — matching food to the communities that need it most, with speed and care.

03
Food Reaches Families

NGOs coordinate pickup and delivery, updating status in real time. You'll know when your donation becomes someone's meal — a moment worth everything.
